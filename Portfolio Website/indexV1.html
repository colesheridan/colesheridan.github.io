About & Contact

I'm Cole Sheridan, an electrical engineer with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering. My work spans the full hardware stack — from analog front-end circuits and power electronics to embedded firmware and PCB layout.

I enjoy the challenge of bridging theory and physical hardware: taking a simulation, laying it out on a PCB, and measuring whether real-world behavior matches the model. When it doesn't, that's where the interesting engineering begins.

I'm currently seeking entry-level EE positions in analog/mixed-signal design, embedded systems, or power electronics. Open to full-time roles and internships.
